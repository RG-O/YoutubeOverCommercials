YouTube™      
Over Commercials




Instructions:

  1. Navigate to the YouTube video, live YouTube video, or YouTube playlist that you would like to play over your stream's commercials.
  2. Copy the video or playlist ID from the end of the URL.
    1. Regular YouTube video example:
      https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5AMQbxBZohY
    2. Live YouTube video example:
      https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QhJcIlE0NAQ
    3. YouTube playlist examples:
      https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=K0HSD_i2DvA&list=PLt982az5t-dVn-HDI4D7fnvMXt8T9_OGB
      https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=K0HSD_i2DvA&list=PLt982az5t-dVn-HDI4D7fnvMXt8T9_OGB&index=1
      https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLt982az5t-dVn-HDI4D7fnvMXt8T9_OGB
  3. Click on the YTOC () chrome extension icon in top right of browser (may need to click puzzle piece icon first to see).
  4. Select mode and then paste ID into the corresponding ID field.
  5. Click "Save" button.
  6. Navigate to whatever video stream you plan to watch on whatever streaming site you use.
  7. Make video full screen.
  8. When commercial begins, click "Ctrl + Alt + C" on keyboard to play YouTube video over the commercial.
  9. When commercial ends, click "Ctrl + Alt + C" on keyboard to hide YouTube video.
  10. Repeat steps #8 and #9 as you enjoy your content!

Notes:

If computer playing through TV, I highly recommend downloading Touch Portal or Deckboard from the app store on your phone and setting up a shortcut for "Ctrl + Alt + C" so you can control this extension on your computer from your phone.

Does not work over top of other youtube videos. (e.g. can't play YouTube videos over top of other YouTube videos)
